Privacy Policy

Noted — Last updated: April 2026

Summary

Noted does not collect, transmit, or share any user data. Everything you create in the app stays on your device.

Data Collection

Noted collects no data whatsoever. No personal information, no analytics, no telemetry, no crash reports, no advertising identifiers, no location data. The app does not have the INTERNET permission and is technically incapable of transmitting data off your device.

Data Storage

All data (notes, images, collections) is stored locally on your device and encrypted at rest using AES-256 (SQLCipher for the database, AES-256-GCM for images). Encryption keys are stored in the Android Keystore (hardware-backed on supported devices). There is no account system, no server, and no cloud component.

Data Sharing

Noted does not share data with any third party. When you use the export or share features, you are explicitly choosing to send your own data to a destination of your choice. Noted never initiates any data transfer on its own.

Backups

Noted opts out of Android's Auto Backup for all sensitive files (database, encryption keys, PIN hash, images). Non-sensitive preferences (theme, view mode) may be included in Android system backups.

Permissions

Camera — to capture photos for note attachments (only when you tap the camera button).
Biometric — to offer fingerprint/face unlock alongside the PIN (only if you enable it).
No other permissions are requested.

Changes

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL with a new date.
